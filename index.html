Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ISBN? +
An ISBN (International Standard Book Number) is a unique 13-digit commercial identifier for books. It allows bookstores, libraries, and distributors to find and track your specific title and edition worldwide.
Is an ISBN required to publish a book? +
If you plan to sell your book in retail stores, libraries, or through major online retailers like Amazon or Barnes & Noble, an ISBN is required. It is the industry standard for book identification.
Will I retain 100% of my book's royalties? +
